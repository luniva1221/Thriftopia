Thriftopia – Terms & Conditions

What You Can Sell

You can only sell or rent clothes on Thriftopia. No shoes, electronics, makeup, or other non-clothing items.

  • Clean
  • In good condition (no tears, major stains, or broken zippers)
  • Owned by you (don’t sell stolen or counterfeit items)

Condition of Items

  • Clothes listed must be freshly washed and ready to wear.
  • Damaged items must be clearly described in the listing (if allowed).
  • You should be honest about size, condition, and brand.

Payments

  • Payments can be made through eSewa or Cash on Delivery (COD).
  • Sellers will get paid once the buyer confirms delivery.
  • Thriftopia may charge a small service fee per transaction.

Delivery & Returns

  • Sellers are responsible for packing and handing over clothes for delivery.
  • Buyers must check the item right away and report any problems within 2 days.
  • If the item is not as described, Thriftopia will help solve the issue.

What’s Not Allowed

  • No selling of non-clothing items (shoes, gadgets, etc.)
  • No selling fake, stolen, or offensive clothing.
  • No spamming or using the site for illegal activities.

Disputes

  • Buyers or sellers can report issues through the Feedback/Help section.
  • Thriftopia will review and try to resolve disputes fairly.
  • If rules are broken, Thriftopia may suspend your account.

Be Responsible

  • You are responsible for what you post and sell.
  • Keep your profile info true and up to date.
  • Treat others with respect.

Changes to Rules

Thriftopia may update these terms from time to time. We’ll notify users of any major changes.
