About Us

Thriftopia is an online thrift platform created by a group of passionate students aiming to promote sustainable fashion in Nepal.

Our mission is to reduce fashion waste and make stylish, second-hand clothing accessible to everyone. We believe in mindful shopping, affordability, and empowering individuals to make eco-friendly choices.

Whether you're here to buy or sell, we’ve built Thriftopia to be your trusted, user-friendly space.

Thank you for being a part of the Thriftopia journey. Together, we’re making fashion better – one reused piece at a time!
